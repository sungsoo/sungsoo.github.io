The Evolution of Intelligent Architectures

Analyzing the convergence of Composite AI and World Models as the primary drivers for future innovation.

Composite AI

An orchestration system that combines multiple AI technologies—generative models, non-generative ML, knowledge graphs, rules/heuristics, and simulations—to assign tasks to the most suitable technology.

Enhanced Accuracy

Superior performance compared to single isolated models.

Explainability

Clearer paths to decision-making via rule-based logic.

Gartner: Go Beyond Single Models Impact Radar: AI

World Models

Learned multi-modal internal representations of an environment, combining modular representations, memory, and action components to simulate and predict future states.

  • Data efficiency and rapid iteration.
  • Safe deployment through simulation.
