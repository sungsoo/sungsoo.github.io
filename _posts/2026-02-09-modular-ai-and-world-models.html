Core R&D Technical Elements

Research Focus 01

Multi-Modal Representations

Focus on Domain Foundation Models (DFMs) that ingest video, sensor inputs, and temporal data to accelerate industry-specific world model capabilities.

Research Focus 02

Physics-Aware Tooling

Differentiable physics and hyper-synthetic data generation to train perception components in safe, scalable virtual environments.

Research Focus 03

Optimized Model Families

Development of SLMs, DSLMs, and LQMs that act as cost-effective modules for larger agentic or world model systems.

Research Focus 04

Sim-to-Real MLOps

End-to-end pipelines that version synthetic scenarios and automate continuous retraining for edge deployment.

Research Focus 05

Trust & TRiSM Research

Runtime protections, provenance detection, and IAM for AI Agents ensuring secure, least-privilege delegation in heterogeneous enterprise environments.
